Santa Over Thrown Through Elvish Uprising in North Pole

After the recent uprisings in the middle east against dictators, the elve's of the north pole were inspired to band together to overthrow a tyrannous dictator: Santa. Why might they rebel? Well it turns out Santa never paid the elves all these years, and they realized they were all a bunch of slaves.

After the over throw of Santa, the eleves have come together led by Sir Washington Elf, Sir Jefferson Elf, and Sir Adams Elf. They have determined that an election will take place for who will be the next Santa.

The election will go in four rounds. There will be 3 nomination rounds where all elves will vote to nominate a one person. Of the 3 nominees, a debate will be held for fellow elve's to choose who to vote for. All elves will then vote for a final person.

Complications arose however. It turns out some of the elves were ... on the NAUGHTY list. With the recent change in political climate of the northpole, they want to band together to try to elect a naughty person to be Santa. The trouble is though, they don't have a majority! They must infiltrate the nice elves to convince them to vote for a naughty elf!

One of the naughty elves is currently trying steal the naughty list to band all naughty people together. Unfortunately, since the nice elves behave, no one stole the nice list for them.
